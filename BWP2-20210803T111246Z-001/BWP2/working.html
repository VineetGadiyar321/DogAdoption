Working



Step 1: After you like one of the dogs from our website , fill up your details( E-mail/PhoneNo).

Step 2: We will contact you to schedule a meet with the dog of your liking . This step is important to understand if the dog is comfortable with you/your family.

Step 3: If the dog is comfortable ,then we will complete the required paperwork. This process can take some time.

Step 4: After you pay a small amount of Adoption Fee ,you can take your fluffy friend to it's new home. This fee is important to us ,as it is used to run our shelter's .
